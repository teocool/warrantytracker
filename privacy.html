Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Warranty Tracker Pro respects your privacy.

Information Collection

WarrantyTracker does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information with third parties.

Receipt Photos and Product Data

Receipt photos, product information, serial numbers, warranty dates, and related content are stored locally on your device and may be synchronized through your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service.

Purchases

Premium purchases are processed securely through Apple's App Store and StoreKit. WarrantyTracker does not receive or store payment information.

Notifications

The app may request permission to send local notifications related to warranty expiration reminders.

Third-Party Services

WarrantyTracker does not use third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking services.
